Location of Kevin's Express Studio

Founded in 2015 by Mr. Anton Alonte, Kevin's Express Studio is a dedicated music studio located at 89 A. Bonifacio St., Barangay Canlalay, City of Biñan, Laguna, Philippines.

Its founder, Mr. Anton Alonte, is the brother of Ronnie Alonte, a well-known Filipino celebrity, bringing both inspiration and recognition to the studio's identity. Over the years, Kevin's Express Studio has become more than just a space — it's a platform where talents are nurtured and music is celebrated.

What started as a small passion project has grown into a trusted space for musicians, aspiring artists, and music enthusiasts in Laguna. The studio offers a range of services including:

  • Studio Rental
  • Recording
  • Drum Lesson

Beyond being a music hub, Kevin's Express Studio is also known for its welcoming community atmosphere, where passion for music and creativity thrives.
